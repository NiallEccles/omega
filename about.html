About

Hi, my name is Niall Eccles.

I am a web designer/developer from Dublin, Ireland.

My CV can be found here, with my relevant experience.

One thing I love about working in this industry is the learning. I never feel like I'm finished learning about a new framework or library. This learning is constantly pushing me to become a better designer and developer.

My first design experience was when I was 15. I was asked to create a set of posters for some gigs along with various other artwork. I never had a drive to go into web until I took part in a program at DCU that taught secondary school students basic web development. Creating websites really spoke to me and interested me.

This factored into my college choices. I studied Creative Digital Media at the Institute of Technology Blanchardstown. There, I learned a wealth of information that spans photography to web. I was most interested in the web modules such as Web Development 1 & 2, Web Design 1 & 2, Mulitmedia Development 1, Content Management Systems, Application Design and Development, UX Design.

During my third year of college I had the opportunity to go on work placement in a web company as part of my work experience. The comapny was called Enhace.ie and is owned by Granite. While I was there I was responsible for updating exisiting websites, ensuring websites were built to the design and testing.
